Here's how to do it.

---

## Eligibility and Rewards

Anyone with an active, paid OptiSigns subscription can use Refer & Earn. This means an account with a [**Standard Plan** or higher](https://www.optisigns.com/pricing). The business you refer must be new to OptiSigns.

When the business you refer signs up to OptiSigns, the following will happen:

* You'll receive $25 in OptiSigns account credit. This applies for each business you refer that applies to a paid plan. So if you refer two businesses, you'll receive $50 - or $25 per business.
* The business you refer also receives $25 in credit.

These credits are applied toward your next invoice.

Your reward is confirmed as soon as the business you referred subscribes to a paid plan. You'll see it as **Pending** on your Refer & Earn page.

To allow for our standard refund period, each credit is held for **14 days** before it becomes available. After this period, the credit moves from Pending to Earned and is applied to your account automatically.

## Frequently Asked Questions

#### Why didn't I receive a credit?

If you were expecting a reward but didn't get one, here are the most common reasons:

* **They haven't subscribed yet**. Rewards are earned only when the business you referred starts a paid plan. Free trials and free plans don't count until they upgrade.
* **It's still pending**. We hold the credits for 14 days after they subscribe.
* **They cancelled or were refunded within 14 days**. Hence why we hold the credits for 14 days after they subscribe. If they cancel or get a refund, the reward is voided for both of you.
* **They didn't subscribe in time**. A referral must lead to a paid subscription within 90 days of sign-up.
* **They weren't a new customer**. Rewards apply only to businesses that are new to OptiSigns.
* **They were already referred by someone else**. Each new customer can be credited to only one referrer.
* **They didn't use your link or invitation**. Make sure they sign up through your referral link or the email invitation you sent.
* **Duplicate sign-up**. To keep things fair, a reward is given only once per customer, per email address, and per company.

If you're still having issues, contact us at support@optisigns.com.

#### Do the businesses I refer get anything?

Yes! They also get $25 in OptiSigns credit toward their first paid invoice.

#### Can I get my reward as cash?

No. Rewards are OptiSigns account credit only — they're applied to your invoices and can't be paid out as cash or transferred.

#### Can I refer myself or use a second account?

No. Self-referrals and duplicate accounts aren't eligible and won't earn credit.
